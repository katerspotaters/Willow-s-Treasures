About Us:

Willow's Treasures is a Mother and Daughter company that features one-of-a-kind wire wrap beaded bracelets, necklaces, and earrings. Each artisan piece is unique and hand-crafted, made with eye catching glass and crystal beads from around the world.

The stunning glass, crystal, and handmade lamp-work beads are found all over the United States, Central America and as far away as Africa and Czechoslovakia.

Everything is lovingly watched over and inspected by our dogs, Willow, who we rescued from Tennessee in March 2012 and Zoey who we rescued from West Virginia in March of 2017!
