About Us:

Willow's Treasures features wire wrap beaded bracelets, earrings and crocheted wire necklaces. Each piece is handmade and unique. There are bracelets and earring sets along with necklaces and matching earrings.

The beautiful glass, crystal, and handmade lamp-work beads that come from all over the world. Everything is lovingly watched over and inspected by our dog, Willow, who we rescued from Tennessee in March 2012!

Our handmade wire wrap bracelets and handmade earrings range from $10 tp $20!
